About LearnX

LearnX is an innovative educational events platform that bridges the gap between institutions and learners. Institutes can post upcoming webinars, seminars, and workshops. Events auto-expire after the scheduled date, making it clutter-free and real-time.

Students can browse, register, and contact event organizers directly. With admin approval and smart features, LearnX makes academic event discovery seamless!
