What is LearnX?

LearnX is a centralized platform built for educational institutions, academies, and training providers to share their upcoming webinars, seminars, and courses with students and professionals. We streamline event visibility, automate expiry after deadlines, and give students direct access to connect and register.

Our Mission

  • To bridge the gap between learners and educational opportunities.
  • To simplify the process of discovering and registering for events.
  • To provide a transparent and reliable system for institutes to manage their outreach.

Key Features

Auto Event Expiry

Events automatically disappear after their end date, keeping the platform clutter-free.

Admin Approval System

Ensures only verified and quality events are visible to users.

Direct Contact & Registration

Students can contact institutions directly and register in a few clicks.

Mobile Friendly Design

Responsive layout ensures seamless experience across all devices.
